Meet the Team

Keep 'em Clean!

Here at Keep 'em Clean Dentistry, we are on a mission to clean and create beautiful smiles, to show off to the world. We are a family based group that formed in order to expand our family through the cleaning of our new friends and neighbors. We love what we do and hope that we can provide the confidence to those around us, by giving them a 'million dollar' shot smile. We promise to make it an easy, fast, and painless experience!
